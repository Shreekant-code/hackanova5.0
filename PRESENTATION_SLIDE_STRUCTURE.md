## SLIDE DESIGN GUIDELINES

### **Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) — Trust, Government
- **Secondary:** Green (#10B981) — Success, Completion
- **Accent:** Orange (#F59E0B) — Energy, Innovation
- **Neutral:** Gray (#6B7280) — Text, secondary info
- **Background:** White or very light gray

### **Layout Rules:**
1. **One idea per slide** (avoid clutter)
2. **Text-heavy slides bad.** Use visuals + speaking
3. **Big font sizes** (title: 40pt, body: 24pt minimum)
4. **High contrast** (dark text on light or vice versa)
5. **Consistent brand** (same fonts throughout)
6. **Real images > stock photos** (show actual product)

### **Typography:**
- **Headings:** Montserrat (bold, modern)
- **Body:** Open Sans (clean, readable)
- Avoid comic sans, papyrus, cursive fonts

### **Visual Elements to Include:**
- 📊 Real data visualizations (not pie charts)
- 🖼️ Screenshots of actual product
- 👥 Real user testimonials (faces, names)
- 🎯 Simple icons for concepts
- 📈 Growth projections (realistic, not hockey stick)

## PRESENTATION DELIVERY TIPS (While Using Slides)

1. **Don't read slides** - Slides are visual anchors, not scripts
2. **Talk to audience, not screen** - Face judges 70% of time
3. **Use slide numbers** for navigation: "This next slide shows..."
4. **Let visuals breathe** - Don't explain every data point (they can read)
5. **Pause after key stats** - "94% first-time approval" [2 sec pause]
6. **Gesture toward slides** - Don't point awkwardly
7. **Use slide transitions minimally** - No spinning or dissolves
8. **Have presenter notes** - Bullet points, not full sentences
9. **Practice with actual slides** - Timing, transitions, speaker view setup

## TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS FOR SLIDES

**Best Options:**
- **Figma** (free, collaborative, modern)
- **Keynote** (if Mac user, most polished)
- **Google Slides** (free, backup if laptop dies)
- **Canva** (easy, templates ready)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Overly animated slides (distracting)
- ❌ Dark slides with light text (hard to read in rooms)
- ❌ Slides with lots of text
- ❌ Unclear charts or graphs
- ❌ Corporate stock photos (clichéd)
